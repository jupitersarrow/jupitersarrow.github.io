Hi! I'm Eden.

I'm the owner and creator of Jupiter's Arrow. For the majority of my life, I've loved creating and building things --- from my childhood toy swords, to the website you're currently browsing. However, when my Grandma Toni passed away, I was left with few things to remember her by. All I had was a silver spoon ring. I remember feeling so connected to this small piece, despite my grandmother's absence, and wondering if I could make something similar. For the next years of my life, I spent hours in my dad's basement perfecting how to make rings that my grandmother would have loved. Every piece I make is made with her in mind --- her heart, her determination, and her undying spirit. 

This one's for you, Toni.

Jupiter's Arrow, 2022.
